About Me

Hi there! I’m a final year PhD candidate in the ECE department at Johns Hopkins University where I work on machine learning for signal processing. Before Hopkins, I graduated with a B.Tech (Hons.) + M.Tech Dual Degree in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras.

I am currently looking for Research Scientist positions in industy starting Summer 2021.

Research Interests

My PhD research focuses on adapting ideas from machine learning and deep learning to improve signal processing. As part of my PhD, I’ve had the opportunity to work on problems in ultrasound imaging, speech, vision, and radar. I am particularly drawn to collaborative signal processing problems like beamforming and compressed sensing, where information from many individual low-quality measurements are combined together intelligently to yield a high quality signal – the whole is greater than the sum of its parts.
